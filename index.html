Early Life of Elon Musk

  • Elon Musk was born 28th June 1971
  • At an early age Musk learnt how to code, which led to him creating and selling his first game "Blastar" at the tender age of 12
  • At 17 Musk travelled to Canada where he enrolled in Queens University to avoid mandatory military service in South Africa
  • During that same year of enrolling at Queens Musk gained a Canadian Citizenship

Education

  • 1992 Studied physics at the University of Pennsylvania
  • Musk graduated with an undergraduates degree in Physics and continued his studies.Later on he completed his second bachelor degree in Economics
  • After leaving Pennsylvania Musk enrolled in Stanford University to pursue a PHD in Energy Physics... 2 Days Later he dropped out to become an entrepreneur.

    • Business Ventures

  • 1995 Founded Zip2 Corporation with brother Kimbal Musk
  • 1999 Zip2 was acquired for $307 Million in cash by Compaq; Musk recieved $22 Million for his 7% share
  • Later 1999 Musk founded X.com which was an online services and e-mail payment company.
  • In 2001 X.com was renamed PayPal and a acquired by eBay in 2002 for $1.5 Billion. Musk recieved $175.8 Million.
